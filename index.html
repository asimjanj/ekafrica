Margin %

Margin is your organiser amount compared to total money in. It shows whether the ceremony itself was worth running, after every cost is paid — arguably the most important number. The higher the better. When operational costs are too high, margin is low. To increase margin, lower operational costs.

Organiser amount %

This is the organiser's amount after the split with co-facilitators, out of leftover revenue once operational costs are paid. It says nothing about whether operational costs were too high.

Margin target

Below 30% is thin, and arguably not worth the time and effort. Consider raising your price or cutting operational costs. Aim for 40% or higher — strong, healthy, and fair for this kind of work.
